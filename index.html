About the Conference

Welcome to the 2026 Biennial Congress of Iberian Perception.

The CIP 2026 will bring together researchers, scientists, and students from around the world to discuss the latest findings in perception science. Set in the beautiful city of Palma, this event promises cutting-edge presentations and networking opportunities.
