About the Conference

Welcome to the 11th Congress of Iberian Perception (CIP 2026).

The CIP 2026 will take place in Palma de Mallorca (Spain) on May 27-29. The event is organized by the Human Evolution and Cognition Group (EvoCog).


The Iberian Conference on Perception is an international conference that brings together researchers who share an interest in understanding the different aspects of Perception. These include Motion Perception, Spatial Vision, Stereopsis, Color Perception, Perception and Action, Attention and Cognition, Auditory Perception, Multisensory Integration, Reading/Speech Perception, and Aesthetic Perception.

Cognitive Science

Exploring the depths of human perception and cognition.

Visual Perception

Latest research in vision, attention, and processing.

Multisensory

Integration of senses and cross-modal perception.

Destination: Palma

Palma is the capital city of Mallorca in the Balearic Islands. It offers a vibrant lifestyle by day and night, brimming with Mediterranean flair, which attracts millions of visitors each year.


Our venue, Sa Riera, is situated in the heart of the city. This prime location places Sa Riera in close proximity to various attractions, shops, restaurants, and cultural activities in the city center.
